Execution Playbook

An industry-grade analytics simulation. Follow this structured 3-week guide to transition from raw datasets to interactive, decision-grade insights.

Duration 3 Weeks
Team Size 6 Students
Data Processing Python
Visualization Tableau

Core Mission Objective

"How can data improve planning, performance, or decision-making in your chosen sector?"

Stage 0: Initiation

Team Roles

  • Project Lead
  • Python/Pandas Lead
  • SciPy/Stats Lead
  • Tableau Lead
  • Documentation Lead
  • Presentation Lead

Domain Options

  • Health & Welfare
  • Defence & Transport
  • Finance & Banking
  • Agriculture & Rural
  • Energy & Environment

Problem Types

  • Performance Analysis
  • Trend & Forecasting
  • Resource Optimization
  • Risk/Anomaly Detection
  • Policy Impact Analysis

Stage 1: Data Foundation

Acquisition & Planning

Days 1 - 2

Finalize your sector and exact problem statement. Acquire a robust dataset relevant to your domain and submit your initial strategy.

Output Required Problem Statement Document, Raw Dataset Link, High-level Analysis Plan.

Data Cleaning (Python)

Days 3 - 4

Import data into a Jupyter Notebook. Handle missing values, remove duplicates, standardize formats, and generate a precise Data Dictionary.

Output Required Cleaned Dataset (CSV/Excel), Cleaning Script (.ipynb), Data Dictionary.

Exploratory Data Analysis

Days 5 - 7

Use Matplotlib/Seaborn to explore distributions and correlations. Identify core KPIs and extract 15-20 meaningful initial insights.

Output Required EDA Jupyter Notebook, Insight Document (Top 15-20 findings).

Stage 2: Insights & Visualization

Advanced Analysis

Days 8 - 10

Perform statistical analysis, segmentation, clustering, time-series forecasting, or root-cause analysis using Python libraries (SciPy/Scikit-learn).

Output Required Advanced Analysis Results & Python Code.

Dashboard Development

Days 11 - 12

Connect clean Python data to Tableau. Build an interactive, professional dashboard featuring both an Executive View and an Operational View.

Output Required Fully Working Tableau Dashboard (Workbook or Public link).

Packaging & Polish

Days 13 - 14

Compile all work into a professional PPT and PDF report summarizing the business problem, methodology, insights, and recommendations.

Final Submission Package Dashboard Link, Final PPT, Final Report (PDF), Datasets, All Scripts.

Stage 3: Evaluation Phase

Viva & Presentation Rules

No project building occurs in Week 3. Teams have 10 minutes to present the PPT and Dashboard, followed by a 5-minute Q&A. The panel may ask any team member to explain any Python code or Tableau logic.

Criteria Evaluated Artifacts Marks
Problem Understanding PPT & Framing 10
Data Cleaning & Quality Python Scripts 15
Analysis Depth Python Stats & EDA 25
Dashboard & Visualization Tableau Workflow 20
Business Recommendations PPT & Report 20
Storytelling & Clarity Live Presentation 10

Rules & Advice

Strict Rules

  • Zero tolerance for plagiarism.
  • No copying logic from other teams.
  • Late submissions incur grade penalties.
  • You must understand your teammate's code.

Top-Tier Expectations

  • Insights beyond simple observation.
  • Dashboards answering specific business queries.
  • Actionable, practical policy recommendations.
  • Immaculately formatted code with comments.
